How it works

From install to first quick paste in under a minute.

FAQ

Does the app read my password fields?
The keyboard only pastes what you choose in the app. It does not automatically read or upload anything you type.


What happens if I uninstall the app?
All snippets stored inside Auto Paste ‚Äì Quick Paste are deleted along with the app. We do not keep any copies.


Can the keyboard send messages by itself?
Auto Send is optional. If enabled, it simply presses the Enter/Send key right after pasting your selected text.


Will you ever add online sync?
If we add cloud features in the future, we will update the app, this website, and the Privacy Policy clearly before you opt-in.
